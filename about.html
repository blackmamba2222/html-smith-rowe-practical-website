SMITH ROWE
about us
Established in 2023 and headquartered in Ottawa, Canada, SMITH ROWE stands at the forefront of the eyewear industry. With our state-of-the-art manufacturing plant located in Toronto, Canada, we proudly design and craft eyeglasses that seamlessly merge fashion and function. Our extensive range of eyeglasses, meticulously produced in Toronto, caters to diverse tastes, styles, and visual requirements. From timeless classics to contemporary innovations, every pair of SMITH ROWE eyeglasses embodies our commitment to exceptional craftsmanship and design excellence. As a Canadian brand, we take pride in our roots and actively contribute to the communities we serve.

Our Ottawa-based customer care team ensures that every customer's experience is marked by personalized attention and expert guidance. We believe that eyewear should not only enhance vision but also celebrate the unique features and personalities of those who wear it. In harmony with our commitment to quality, we uphold environmentally conscious practices throughout our operations. From sourcing sustainable materials to employing eco-friendly production methods, we strive to minimize our environmental impact and promote a greener future for generations to come. SMITH ROWE is more than an eyewear company; it's a symbol of authenticity, innovation, and positive change. Join us in discovering eyeglasses that not only sharpen your vision but also reflect your individuality. As we proudly produce these eyeglasses in Toronto and extend our influence from Ottawa, we invite you to be part of a movement that transforms how you perceive both yourself and the world around you.
